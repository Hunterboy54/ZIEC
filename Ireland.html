About Ireland

According to the QS World University RankingsÂ® 2019, one of the 14 institutes of technology and all seven of Ireland's public universities are placed in the top 700 universities in the world. Seven private colleges exist in Ireland, ranging in size from the modest Burren College of Art, which enrols only 200 students, to the sizable Dublin Business School, which enrols over 9,000. Universities, specialised colleges (which provide programmes in a single subject area), and technological institutions make up Ireland's higher education system. The latter provide a wide choice of vocational programmes in fields like hospitality, healthcare, pharmacy, media, textiles, marketing, and many more in addition to technical courses.
