Why Choose ZIEC?

The company ZENITH IMMMIGRATION AND EDUCATION CONSULTANCY offers career and educational solutions that help the students in knowing their inherent strengths, choose their “best-fit” careers and get a correct career assessment. The organization founded by Hardik Thakkar provides a detailed insight into the requirements the students have and provide a clear understanding of problems that, if unaddressed, cause major difficulties in the future.

We are here to identify every student’s unique quality and give the best-suited career advice to them. We are here to cater to every situation that a student face to choose his/her dream career. We take all the necessary steps to make parents and the students feel confident and informed about the career path they finally plan to tread on.
