About Canada

Canada has rapidly become one of the most sought after countries for international students and professionals hoping to immigrate owing to its good universities and abundant resources. The country has abundant natural resources and a multifarious environment. The capital city, Ottawa, along with cities like Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Hamilton and Regina are some of the most populated and liveable cities in the country. Canada has cemented its place as a strong supporter of arts. The country holds various festivals to celebrate music, dance and sports.

Why Study in canada ?

  • No# 1 Country to live in (as per The United Nations)
  • Cost of education is lowest amongst developed countries
  • Vibrant economy and work force
  • Easy student visa application requirement
  • Post study work after studies
  • Permanent Residency options after study and work
  • Degrees anci Diplomas awarded by Colleges/ Universities have international recognition and acceptance
